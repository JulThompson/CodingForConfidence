Lesson Plans

Module 2: Girls Code

Overview

In this session, teams begin to explore coding using HTML. Teams will continue to engage in team building activities to develop their interdependence and enhance their communication and collaboration skills. This time they will engage in Pair Programming on the next level, incorporating words into their sharing, the next step toward their goal of symbolic representations in coding and writing to solve a problem. Next, through a Code Along activity, the girls will code a webpage using HTML, and then have time to explore and experiment with HTML.
